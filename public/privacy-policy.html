Privacy Policy

Lock In Fitness App

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Lock In is a fitness tracking and social accountability app. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is protected.

1. Who We Are

Lock In is operated as an independent app available at lockin-app-psi.vercel.app. If you have any questions about this policy, use the Contact / Feedback option in the app.

2. What Data We Collect

We only collect data that is necessary to provide the app's features:

  • Account information — your email address and password (used for login only)
  • Profile information — your display name, bio, age, sex, weight, height, and activity level (provided voluntarily to calculate calorie needs)
  • Fitness data — workouts you log, including exercise name, duration, and calories burned
  • Nutrition data — meals you log, including food name, calories, and macros
  • Social data — match requests you send or receive, chat messages with matched users, and activity feed posts
  • App preferences — your goal, calorie targets, macro targets, and display settings (stored locally on your device)

We do not collect payment information, precise location data, contacts, or any data from your device beyond what you explicitly enter.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide core app features — calorie tracking, workout logging, macro calculations
  • To match you with other users based on fitness level and goal
  • To display your activity in the social feed to your matched connections
  • To calculate your TDEE and suggest calorie targets
  • To maintain your XP, level, and streak across sessions

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your data with third parties except as described below.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a SOC 2 compliant database provider. All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS. Row-Level Security (RLS) policies ensure that each user can only access their own data — no other user can read, edit, or delete your information.

Your email and password are managed by Supabase Auth and are never accessible to the app directly.

5. What Other Users Can See

When you use Lock In's social features, other users may see:

  • Your display name, bio, avatar, fitness goal, and level badge on the Discover tab
  • Your workout posts in the activity feed (visible only to your matched connections)
  • Messages you send in chat (visible only to the matched user you're chatting with)

Your email address, weight, height, age, and detailed nutrition data are never visible to other users.

6. Third-Party Services

Lock In uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and real-time messaging. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Vercel — app hosting. Vercel Privacy Policy
  • Open Food Facts — food nutrition database (search queries only, no personal data sent). Open Food Facts Terms

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, submit a deletion request using the Contact / Feedback option in the app and we will permanently delete your data within 30 days.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data and account
  • Export your data in a readable format

To exercise any of these rights, use the Contact / Feedback option in the app.

9. Children's Privacy

Lock In is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. California and CCPA

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete it, and the right to opt out of its sale. We do not sell personal information. For requests, use the Contact / Feedback option in the app.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Use the Contact / Feedback option inside the app.
